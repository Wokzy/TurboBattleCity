# TurboBattleCity
Battle City PC impl

## Controls
### Keyboard
---
 - `arrows` - movement
 - `space` - shoot
 - `shift` - boost
 - `r` - reload
### Xbox 360 Controller
---
- `left axis` or `hat` - movement
- `A` - shoot
- `B` - boost
- `X` - reload
### Sony Dualshock 4
---
- `left axis` - movement
- `x` - Shoot
- `circle` - boost
- `square` - reload
